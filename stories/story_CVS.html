CVS

GraceEvelyn reads the receipt one more time but she shakes her head. "No," she says, "No--see here you charged me for two when there should have been one."

"But ma'am you did purchase two."

"No--yes--but," GraceEvelyn shakes her head in utter dismay. "I did yes but, they were on sale, see?" She holds up the bottle of shampoo.

The cashier who lost her nametag leans in. "Doesn't say anything about a sale."

"I know that."

"Then why're you showing it to me?"

GraceEvelyn points behind her. She has perfect posture and a strong, straight arm. "Go check for yourself! And see if it's on sale!"

The unnamed cashier shrugs. "Want me to refund you the charge?"

GraceEvelyn drops her arm. "That'll do. Yes."

"I'll have to ring you up again. It'll take a minute."

"Do what you must."

"I'll have to pretend to the system that you're making a return."

"That's okay. I can manage."

"It may reflect poorly on your CCS."

"My what?"

"Customer Coupon Status."

"Excuse me?"

"It may not. But it may."

"Why would--why would that reflect poorly--?"

The unnamed cashier shrugs. "I don't make the rules, ma'am. Do you want to go through with the return?"

GraceEvelyn shakes her head furiously. She looks at the shampoo. She opens her eyes wide. "Oh!"

"What's that ma'am?"

"This isn't even the right scent!"

"Would you like to make a return?"

"Will that reflect poorly on my CCS?"

"It could, ma'am. But maybe not."

GraceEvelyn puffs out her chest. "I'll take the risk." She hands the shampoo to the cashier, who scans it.

"Would you like to get a replacement bottle?"

"Yes...I...would you mind? Waiting?"

The cashier yawns. "Not at all, ma'am. My shift lasts 10 hours and i have 7 hours remaining. I'll be here, in this spot, for the remainder of that time. I have no problems making the rest of the line wait for you to pick out whichever scent of shampoo you desire. I have no attachment to this corporation or the wellbeing of anybody but the person who is currently at my register. When you are my customer, you are my sole focus--I exist to serve you. I find it significant that my purpose is transient, shifting with each personality who enters my life."

"Thank you, dear, thank you. I'll be right back. One moment--so sorry for all the trouble. You really are such a doll!"
